Ingredients:
 - 1/5 kg peleed not salty peanuts  
 - 3 spicy dried chiles
 - 1/2 onion
 - 1 garlic
 - 3 tomates


Directions:
 1. In a pan toast the penanuts, add the chiles.
 2. Put the peanuts on the blender.
 3. Fried the tomatos until they are soft.
 4. Blend the tomatos, onions and garlic.
 5. In a pot put the mix until it boil. 
 6. Add the the crushed peanuts and stir for 15min at low temperature.
